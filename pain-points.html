Overwhelming Volume of Security Alerts
Pain Point: It is common for organizations to struggle with an overwhelming volume of security alerts and false positives, leading to "alert fatigue" for their security teams.
Solution: Trellix’s XDR solution combines data across multiple security layers, filtering out false positives and prioritizing high-risk alerts. Trellix's XDR uses AI-driven analytics to reduce workload and improves detection accuracy. Using AI-driven analytics, Trellix reduces the amount of alerts that security teams have to deal with.
Incomplete Visibility Across Attack Surfaces
Pain Point: Limited visibility and blind spots are common within many organizations' systems, which attackers can exploit.
Solution: Trellix’s Threat Intelligence and XDR Platform provides an integrated view across endpoints, networks, and cloud resources, offering visibility into threats. By combining data from multiple sources with threat intelligence feeds, Trellix allows security teams to identify and respond to threats that may otherwise go undetected.
Insufficient Threat Intelligence
Pain Point: A lack of up-to-date threat intelligence hinders organizations from being proactive in protecting against emerging threats,
Solution: Trellix offers Threat Intelligence as a service, providing real-time updates on emerging threats. This service allows organizations to make proactive security decisions and adjust defenses in real time. Trellix integrates intelligence feeds into its XDR platform, enhancing detection capabilities.
Difficulty in Detecting and Responding to Sophisticated Threats
Pain Point: Traditional security tools often struggle with advanced, multi-stage attacks that evolve and spread quickly.
Solution: Trellix’s Advanced Threat Protection (ATP) and Machine Learning Algorithms allow for proactive threat hunting as well as continuous monitoring. Trellix identifies unusual patterns, reducing time to detect and stop complex threats and providing strong defense against sophisticated attackers.
